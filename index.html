How to Play

  • Click red circles to earn time and rack up points. Smaller circles are worth more.
  • Some circles glow green; right-click them. Left-clicking a green circle costs you points.
  • Your timer drains faster as difficulty ramps up. Use the Max Difficulty slider to set the cap.
  • When time runs out your points are multiplied by the current difficulty to form your final score.
